Receive Data from Serial Device

Instructions

  • Make sure your browser supports WebSerial. Most of the chromium based browsers do. If your browser does not support it, you may need to enable experimental features or use a different browser.
  • Select desired Baud rate and a pop-up window will appear.
  • Select the external Serial Device and press Connect.
  • Type your message and choose the endline type.
  • Press Send or hit Enter to transmit.
  • View sent and received data below.
  • If the device disconnects, status will turn red.
